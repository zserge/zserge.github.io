My advent of code

Ho, ho, ho! Ah, sorry, it’s not Christmas yet. But the Advent season has already started and plenty of kids have already stuck their teeth into chocolates from the first door of the advent calendar box.

I like my treats to be bitter-sweet, so instead I decided to go for a small challenge. I am a musician myself, as you may have guessed from the previous post. However, friendly would probably describe me rather as a collector of musical instruments. I want to learn new instruments, but I struggle with finding notes and tunes to play. So, to scratch my own itch and help other musicians who don’t play their instruments well or can’t read notes - I would like to start a universal tablature project. I want to build a TaaS (tablature as a service) that would allow users to search for popular songs and see tablatures for their musical instrument of preference.

Day 1

A friend of mine came with a great name: LaLaTabs.

Playful, naive, friendly, and descriptive. Fits well. So I got a domain immediately and put an ugly landing page there. Not much progress so far, but brainstorming the features and planning the project. I collected all the garbage I got on my desk to make a photo that I used for the background image.

Here is the link: https://lalatabs.com/
